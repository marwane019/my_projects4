JD ↔ CV Matcher (Browser)

Paste a Job Description and your CV text. We compute similarity and list missing keywords — all in your browser (no server).

How it works

We tokenize, drop stopwords, compute TF counts, and estimate cosine similarity on term-frequency vectors. Missing keywords are top terms in JD absent from CV.
